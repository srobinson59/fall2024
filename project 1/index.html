Diary of a Wimpy Kid by Jeff Kinney


This series follows Greg Heffley through his growing pains as a preteen. Greg and his best friend, Rowley, go on fun adventures and even have a few arguments throughout the series. Greg also faces issues with his older brother, Roderick. The books were so popular that they were later turned into movies.


"The first book in the series was published in 2007 and became instantly popular for its relatable humor. Today, more than 290 million copies have been sold around the world!"
