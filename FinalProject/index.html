My name is Savannah Robinson. I am a senior New Media and Digital Design Major at Fordham University with a Visual Arts Minor and a concentration in Art, Text, and Design. My first two years of college I was a psychology major and I started to make the switch to the artistic space in the last semester of my sophomore year. Throughout my junior and senior years at Fordham I have taken the following courses that relate to my major, minor, and concentration:
  • Visual Thinking
  • Drawing 1
  • Ethical Issues in Media
  • Introduction to Video Game Design
  • Creative Coding
  • Explorations in New Media and Digital Design
  • Digital Production for New Media
  • User Experience Design: Design for Empowerment
  • Graphic Design and Digital Tools
  • Explorations in Digital Storytelling
Most recently, this semester I am taking Logos, Branding, and Presentation and Fundamentals of Website Design. Looking to the future, next semester, I am taking Visual Effects and Motion Graphics, and Representation in Art.
