Fundamentals of Web Design!

Exercise 1 was our introductory website. It taught us the basics of code. Exercise 2 was our second website, where we formatted and provided examples of the 10 Principles of Good Design. In Exercise 3 we had to provide our analysis of the readings from that point in the semester. Exercise 4 takes a look at responsiveness, so resizing the browser should trigger some change on the website. Project 1 was our first out of class assignment in which we were tasked with making a website about a book. Project 2's goal was to analyze and critique a website of our choosing. Project 3 was a narrative project. This website is Project 4, a website that takes a look at all of our work from this semester.
